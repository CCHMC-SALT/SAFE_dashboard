Cincinnati Meal Gap (DRAFT)

  • The meal gap calculation is a measure aimed at deciphering where people may be coming up short on having enough meals. We believe the 4 main sources of meals are personal income, governmental assistance (SNAP), free school lunches (Cincinnati Public Schools[CPS]) and non-profits. In these charts we are estimating how much each source provides to a community and then examining where and when there may not be enough meals.

Points appearing in the green section, with values less than zero, indicate a meal surplus for that month
